How to reply to an email

Respond to email that appear in your inbox. Communication is key!

You can either Reply to, Reply All or Forward an email.

Reply
Replies to the sender of the email.

Reply All
Replies to the sender and CC recipients of the email.

Forward
Forwards the email to new recipients.

You can use:

  • Button on tool bar
  • Option on menu bar
  • Pop up menu (right-click)
